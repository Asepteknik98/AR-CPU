- Black and white
- Square shape
- Distinctive corners

2. Create 200x200 pixel image
3. Fill with black (#000000)
4. Copy pattern from: https://github.com/AR-js-org/AR.js/blob/master/data/images/HIRO.png
5. Or use generator: https://jeromeetienne.github.io/AR.js/three.js/examples/marker-training/examples/generator.html
